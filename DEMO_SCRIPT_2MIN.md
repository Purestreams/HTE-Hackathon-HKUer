# 2‑Minute Spoken Demo Script (Lighthouse: EduAI)

Time-coded **talk track** for a ~2 minute live presentation.

## Pre-demo (10s, not counted)
“What you’re seeing is Lighthouse: EduAI. It’s a session-scoped workflow that turns course materials into study-ready outputs: organized sources, generated practice, guided review, and shareable results.”

---

## 0:00–0:10 — One-sentence pitch
“Lighthouse turns messy course materials into a **session-scoped** library, generates practice content, runs a **consensus-style validation** with transparent review, and lets you ask questions and export results for study and sharing.”

## 0:10–0:25 — Sessions
“The core idea is **sessions**. Each course gets its own isolated workspace, so your sources, snapshots, and AI outputs never get mixed across classes. That makes iterative study workflows safe and repeatable.”

## 0:25–0:40 — Upload + Smart PDF ingest
“On the ingestion side, Lighthouse handles a wide range of document formats and layouts. It aims to preserve structure and readability so your materials remain usable for studying, generating practice, and Q&A.”

## 0:40–1:00 — Mockpaper generation
“Once sources are in the session, we generate practice content directly from those materials. You can tune the difficulty and style, and get both questions and solutions so it’s immediately usable for revision.”

## 1:00–1:25 — Validate (Consensus Engine)
“Validation is the flagship. Instead of trusting a single pass, Lighthouse runs a **consensus-style review** that checks the content for issues and produces a revised version. The review is transparent, so you can follow what changed and why.”

## 1:25–1:40 — Chat (RAG over session files)
“Then there’s a chat layer over your chosen session files. You can ask for explanations, summaries, or new practice questions. Importantly, responses are grounded in your selected materials rather than generic answers.”

## 1:40–1:55 — Library + PDF export
“Everything you generate and ingest is browsable in the library. You can view your materials, open original documents, and export polished outputs for sharing or printing.”

## 1:55–2:00 — Snapshots + Jobs
“Finally, you can snapshot your workspace state to iterate safely, and you get clear progress visibility for longer-running tasks—so the workflow stays reliable end-to-end.”

---

## 60-second version (optional)
“Session-scoped library in. Practice content out. Transparent validation to improve quality. Chat grounded in your materials. Export shareable results. Snapshots and progress tracking to keep everything safe and reliable.”
